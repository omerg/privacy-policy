## Getting Started

Download Lie Detector from your device's app store:

## Features

### Voice Analysis
The app analyzes voice patterns including:
- Pitch variations
- Speech rate changes
- Voice tremors
- Pauses and hesitations

**How to Use:**
1. Tap the "Voice Analysis" button
2. Press record and ask your question
3. Record the response (minimum 10 seconds recommended)
4. Wait for analysis results

### Facial Expression Analysis
Uses your device camera to analyze:
- Micro-expressions
- Eye movement patterns
- Facial muscle tension
- Blinking rate

**How to Use:**
1. Tap "Face Analysis" mode
2. Position the subject's face within the frame
3. Start recording during questioning
4. Review the analysis report

### Combined Analysis
For best results, use both voice and facial analysis together:
1. Enable "Combined Mode" in settings
2. Position camera to capture face and enable microphone
3. Record your session
4. Receive comprehensive analysis

### History & Reports
- View past analyses in the History section
- Export reports in PDF format
- Share results (subject to privacy settings)
- Organize sessions with custom tags and notes

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Open or Crashes
- Ensure your device OS is up to date
- Clear app cache: Settings > Apps > Lie Detector > Clear Cache
- Restart your device
- Reinstall the app if issue persists

### Microphone Not Working
- Check that microphone permission is granted
- Ensure no other app is using the microphone
- Test your device microphone in another app
- Try removing any protective case that may block the mic

### Camera Issues
- Verify camera permission is enabled
- Clean your camera lens
- Close other apps that may be using the camera
- Ensure adequate lighting for facial analysis

### Analysis Not Accurate or Not Loading
- Record for at least 10-15 seconds for better accuracy
- Ensure clear audio without background noise
- Position face clearly within frame for facial analysis
- Check your internet connection (required for cloud processing)
- Wait for processing to complete (may take 10-30 seconds)

### App Running Slowly
- Close background apps to free up memory
- Ensure you have at least 500MB free storage
- Disable real-time analysis if device is older
- Update to the latest app version

## FAQ

### Is this a real lie detector?
The app analyzes voice patterns, facial expressions, and physiological indicators that may suggest deception. However, **results are for entertainment and informational purposes only** and should not be considered definitive evidence. Accuracy depends on many factors and is not 100% reliable.

### How accurate is the analysis?
Accuracy varies based on:
- Recording quality
- Subject's baseline behavior
- Environmental conditions
- Type of questions asked

The app should not be used for legal, medical, or critical decision-making.

### Do I need an internet connection?
- **Setup:** Internet required
- **Local Processing:** Works offline
- **Cloud Processing:** Internet required
- **Account Sync:** Internet required

### Can I use this on others without permission?
**No.** You must obtain explicit consent from anyone you analyze. Recording others without consent may violate privacy laws in your jurisdiction. Always respect privacy and legal requirements.

### Is my data shared with others?
We do not sell your data. Limited data may be shared with service providers (hosting, analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements. See our [Privacy Policy](index.html) for details.

### How do I delete my data?
1. Go to Settings > Privacy > Delete My Data
2. Or contact support at privacy@lieddetectorapp.com
3. Data deletion is usually completed within 30 days

### Can I export my analysis results?
Yes! Go to History > Select Analysis > Export > Choose PDF or CSV format.

### Why are there in-app purchases?
The basic app is free with core features. Premium features include:
- Advanced analysis algorithms
- Unlimited history storage
- Ad-free experience
- Priority processing
- Export features

### Does the app work on tablets?
Yes! The app is optimized for both phones and tablets on iOS and Android.
