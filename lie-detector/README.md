# Privacy Policy - Lie Detector

_Last Updated: January 17, 2026_

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Lie Detector ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Device Information

We may collect information about your device, including:

- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system and version
- Device identifiers (IDFA, Android Advertising ID)
- Device performance metrics

### 2.2 Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with our App, including:

- Features used and frequency of use
- Time and duration of app sessions
- Crashes and technical errors
- Pages or content accessed

### 2.3 Voice and Audio Data

Our App analyzes voice patterns and audio input to provide lie detection features:

- Data is processed locally on your device whenever possible.
- It may be temporarily stored in memory for analysis.
- It is not permanently stored on our servers by default.
- Data may be transmitted to our secure servers only with your explicit consent.

### 2.4 Camera and Biometric Data

If you grant permission, we may access:

- Camera data for facial analysis features
- Facial expressions and eye movement patterns
- Biometric indicators (pulse, heart rate if available through device sensors)

### 2.5 Personal Information

We may collect personal information you voluntarily provide, such as:

- Name and email address (for account creation)
- User-created content within the app
- Contact information for customer support

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- Providing and improving the App's lie detection functionality
- Personalizing your experience
- Analyzing app performance and fixing technical issues
- Conducting research and development
- Sending service-related notifications
- Complying with legal obligations
- Preventing fraud and ensuring security

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Processing

The Lie Detector App prioritizes your privacy by processing most data locally on your device. Analysis of voice and facial data occurs on your device whenever possible, minimizing data transmission.

### 4.2 Server Storage

Any data transmitted to our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols. We maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access or disclosure.

### 4.3 Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.
